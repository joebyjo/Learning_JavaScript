Grandma's Famous Salmon Cakes

This a recipe for my Grandmother's famous salmon cakes.It is a family recipe that has been passed down
through generations. Serve with macaroni and cheese or any other type of side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, drained and flaked
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Pick through the salmon and remove any bones.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and add the diced onion, salmon and pepper.
  3. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Shape into 2 ounce patties; about 7 or 8 patties.
  5. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat some oil.
  6. Fry each patty for 5 minutes on each side or until crispy and golden brown.
